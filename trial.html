Our Tours

Explore our range of tours — each designed to show a different side of the Lake District.

Six Lakes Splendour

Half-day tour exploring six of the Lake District’s most distinctive waters. A relaxed journey through contrasting landscapes — from open vistas to quiet shorelines — offering a true sense of the region’s natural variety and character.

Beatrix Potter

Half-day tour visiting the landscapes and villages that inspired Beatrix Potter’s timeless tales. Explore charming scenes, quiet lanes, and places that still reflect her love for the Lake District and its natural beauty.

Wordsworth's Lakeland

Half-day tour following in the footsteps of William Wordsworth. Visit the landscapes, homes, and viewpoints that shaped his poetry — a journey through the scenery that defined the Romantic vision of the Lake District.

About

Tour The Lakes offers scenic journeys through the heart of the Lake District — comfortable, personal, and thoughtfully paced. Each route is accompanied by carefully recorded commentary, sharing local insight and stories as you travel. It’s not a guided tour in the traditional sense, but a relaxed way to discover the region — consistent, clear, and crafted for those who value the quiet side of exploration.
